Our Aim

Moving Enterpreneurs to greater heights

*

Become A Hub

To be a hub for emerging and established women enterpreneurs, company executives and professionals who would like to take her businesses careers and lives to the next level.

*

To Empower

To empower, educate, inspire, coach and mentor likeminded women through a series of interactive workshops that happen yearly in all major provinces starting with Gauteng, followed by Kwazulu Natal, then the Western Cape, Eastern Cape, Free State and Mpumalanga, Northwest Province and Limpopo may join the Gauteng province summit.

*

Carefully Selective

To carefully select relevent and successful business people and expertise in their fields who will be excellent role models who share the same vision of uplifting other enterpreneurs through their challenges and experiences.

  • Sessions for corporate and businesses: To offer sessions for corporate and businesses leaders looking to effectively succeed in today's rapidly changing and recession threatened markets
  • Learning platform: To provide a learning platform and networking for these enterpreneurs.
  • Business made simpler: Attendees will discover simpler ways to doing business and create new opportunities for growth.
  • Corporate governance: Offer events that inspire transperent accountability, ethics, morals and general productive operation of companies, through excellent corporate governance policies.
