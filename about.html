COMPANY HISTORY

Success Summit is the brain child of Dr Nondumiso Mzizana, thecurrent businesswoman of the year, entrepreneur category in
South Africa.

It was founded in 2010. After winning this prestigious award, Dr Mzizana felt the need to leave a legecy by imparting the knowledge and her experiences she gained over 10 years of running her succesful medical, dental and pharmaceutical suppliers business which is the biggest medical equipment company that is 100 per cent female owned in the country 

MISSION

Empowering emerging entrepreneurs and established from different cultural black groups.

VISION

To enable entrepreneurs to be more creative and innovative - to make the best of their potential.

COMPANY AWARDS

The founder of this initiative, Dr Nondumiso Mzizana, is the BWASA business woman of the year 2011/2012

Development

PERSONAL AND BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT
Success Summit is about personal and business self development. We wish to enable a new set of entrepreneurs who will not have to depend on employment of survival, and thus create more jobs in the process

Building Enterprises

BUILDING SUCCESSFUL ENTERPRISES
Our focus is on building successful enterprises. We will focus on helping people with enterpreneurial skills, leadership skills, marketing, innovation and sales skills. We will be delivering training programs focusing on communications skills, presentation skills, referral marketing, networking skills, coaching, mentoring and more.
