COMPANY HISTORY

Success Summit is the brain child of Dr Nondumiso Mzizana, thecurrent businesswoman of the year, entrepreneur category in

After winning this prestigious award, Dr Mzizana felt the need to leave a legecy by imparting the knowledge and her experiences she gained over 10 years of running her succesful medical, dental and pharmaceutical suppliers business which is the biggest medical equipment company that is 100 per cent female owned in the country
